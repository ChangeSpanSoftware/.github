# ChangeSpanSoftware Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

ChangeSpanSoftware is committed to a respectful, inclusive, professional, and evidence-focused engineering environment.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate clearly and respectfully.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Document assumptions, risks, and tradeoffs.
- Protect confidential project, customer, and security information.
- Give credit for contributions.
- Raise safety, integrity, or security concerns promptly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate misinformation, disclosure of confidential information, and behavior that compromises project or user safety are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to `eaguiar@change-span.com`. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and addressed proportionately.
